Omega Watches is a Swiss brand founded in 1848, synonymous with watchmaking excellence, precision and innovation. The brand is a major actor of the luxury watches industry.

In 2015, I worked as the lead digital art director, in collaboration with Publicis Nurun, on a tender proposal to define their online positioning and design their new responsive website; our proposal was selected.

The website was thought and designed as a ‘mobile first’ to fit the users’ habits. I was also in charge of the production design process and managed a web designer.
