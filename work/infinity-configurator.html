Infinity

Car Configurator

To proof the vision of a truly unique car configurator concept, a little prototype was created using high quality renderings done by the amazing animation department at Parasol Island.

Try the prototype

The idea was to create a seamless transition when switching between the configuration of the car's interior and exterior. Serving a view respectively to the one you are configuring already worked well on paper and even the final result was not just visually appealing but provided a good way to configure and compare the interior and exterior of an infinity car at the same time.
