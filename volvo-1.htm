Volvo Car Group is a global Swedish brand created in 1927 which produces a premium range of cars. The brand mission is to build the strongest and most innovative cars. Volvo has placed innovation, design and ergonomy at the center of their concern to offer the easiest life for people.
 
In 2017, I worked as the lead art director with Octave & Octave, on the competition to design their new responsive corporate website. 

Once we won, i worked with Antoine Barres (junior art director) on the production design process.
