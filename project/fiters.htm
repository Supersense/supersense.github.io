Fiters is an online service platform allowing users to book personal trainer for a fitness session anywhere and anytime they want.

The startup is based in Station F Paris. 

In 2016, I worked directly with the client as creative director to think and design the brand identity, their responsive design website and 2 versions of the app: the user app and the professional app dedicated to the coaches.

I worked on the UX, UI and design of the website and the apps.

As I was in charge of the brand identity, I also developed the merchandising aspect:  kakemono, print ads, t-shirts, bags etc…
